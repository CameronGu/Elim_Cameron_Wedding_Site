Our Story

Cameron Guthrie is an entrepreneur and early-adopter of the digital nomad lifestyle. For more than a decade, he’s lived in faraway tropical places, building companies that leverage the skills of his fellow digital nomads to create unique value for his clients. His Travel Platform Management company, Already There, manages contractor logistics of large projects for some of the biggest companies in the world.
